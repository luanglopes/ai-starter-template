Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 11, 2026

1. Introduction

AI Starter is operated by LGL Tecnologia. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our platform.

AI Starter is a productivity platform that helps you manage todos with AI-enhanced descriptions. You create tasks, and AI transforms rough notes into clear, actionable descriptions. By using AI Starter, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and password (securely hashed; we never store plain-text passwords)
  • Todo data — the tasks you create, including titles, descriptions, and completion status
  • Usage data — features used, AI enhancement usage, and session information
  • Billing information — payment details processed securely through our payment provider (we do not store your card number)

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Provide the todo management service — store and manage your tasks across devices
  • Generate AI-enhanced descriptions — send your todo titles and descriptions to AI providers to generate improved, actionable descriptions
  • Process billing — manage subscriptions and payments
  • Improve service quality — analyze usage patterns to enhance AI enhancement accuracy and feature relevance
  • Send transactional emails — deliver account verification emails and password resets

4. Third-Party Services

AI Starter integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Cloudflare — for hosting, CDN, and application delivery (Workers)
  • AI providers (Google Gemini) — for generating enhanced todo descriptions from your task data
  • Supabase — for database hosting (Postgres)
  • Payment provider — for secure payment processing and subscription management

5. Data Storage & Security

We take the security of your data seriously:

  • The application is hosted on Cloudflare Workers with edge-level security
  • Database is hosted on Supabase Postgres with encryption at rest
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Passwords are securely hashed and never stored in plain text

6. Cookies

AI Starter uses session cookies for authentication purposes. These cookies are:

  • HTTP-only — not accessible via JavaScript
  • Secure — transmitted only over HTTPS
  • Same-site — restricted to prevent cross-site request forgery

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any third-party analytics cookies.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Export your data — download your account information
  • Delete your data — request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Update your information — modify your account details through the application settings

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email address below or use the account settings within the application.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Active accounts — your data is retained for as long as your account is active
  • Deleted accounts — upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days
  • Billing records — transaction records may be retained as required by law

9. AI-Generated Content

AI Starter uses artificial intelligence to enhance todo descriptions. AI-generated descriptions are suggestions to help you write clearer tasks. The AI may occasionally produce inaccurate or irrelevant content. You can always edit or discard AI-enhanced descriptions.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. For material changes, we will notify you via email at the address associated with your account. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
